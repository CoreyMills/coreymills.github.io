UTC Game

Here is a Game Jam that was run out of the UTC@MediaCityUK college in 2017, the theme being the college.

I created two small 2D mini-games that played one after the other, The first a parody of Donkey Kong and the second of Pac-Man. The enemy characters were based on college lecturers at the time.

​The first Image shows the Kong Level and the second is a short video of the games being roughly played. Created with Unity.
